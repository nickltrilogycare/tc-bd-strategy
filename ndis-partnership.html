Key Messaging Pillars

NDIS Sector Pressure

600+ SC closures since Jul 2024. Rates frozen 7 years (25% real-terms cut). 81% can't deliver at current prices. Navigator model replacing SCs by ~2028.

⚠️ Rule: Never lead with doom. Frame as "secure your future" + "additional revenue stream". Mirror frustration only if they volunteer it first. — Andrew Wallace

Trilogy Value Proposition

2nd largest provider. $340M govt funding. 300+ CP network. % revenue model (scales, no cap). We carry registration & compliance. 45-min annual phone audit. We provide clients from day one.

"Elderly Parents" Angle

The single most effective talking point (per Andrew Wallace): "Your NDIS clients have elderly parents — many on aged care packages. You're one conversation away from 20 clients."

"Quick question — how many of your NDIS participants have elderly parents? Because in our experience, that's where your first 20 aged care clients come from. You already know the family."

Revenue Comparison

NDIS SC: $5K/client (50hrs, zero margin) vs Aged Care CP: $6.8K/client (4-6hrs). 50 clients = $188-282K/yr. Rate grows automatically with govt funding.

"We Provide Clients"

Removes the #1 barrier to action. Trilogy allocates participants to new CPs. "You won't start from zero." Plus their own NDIS client families (elderly parents) are immediate leads.
